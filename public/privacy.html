1. Introduction

Assessly (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our web application available at https://www.assesslyft.com.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information beyond what is provided by Google for authentication.

When you connect your Google Analytics account, we temporarily access:

  • Your Google Analytics 4 account ID
  • Selected metrics (e.g., views, sessions, conversions)

We do not store access tokens or your data on any external server. Everything is processed securely in-browser or via secure serverless functions on Vercel.

3. Use of Google OAuth

Our application uses Google OAuth solely to allow you to connect your Google Analytics account and retrieve metrics for analysis.

We comply with Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We do not use cookies or third-party tracking services.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any of your data on our servers. If you disconnect Google Analytics, your access token is removed from the browser immediately.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke access at any time via your Google Account Permissions.
